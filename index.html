The Style Helper

A dream come true for authors and editors alike.

The Style Helper is software designed to help you as a writer (or editor) of creative writing. It analyzes features of writing such as sentence length and vocabulary to help you identify the strengths and weaknesses of your writing style and learn how to make your novel or short story the best that it can be!
The Style Helper is just that: a helper. You should NOT apply every suggestion it gives you! You're the writer, after all. If you disagree with advice that the Style Helper gives, then trust yourself.

Some things the style helper can do:

  • Help you identify 'problem words' such as very, really, and literally
  • Point out repeated words and phrases that you may want to cut back on
  • Identify places where your sentence length might need to be more varied
Some things the style helper cannot do:
  • Tell you what to do! You're smarter than this thing, by far.
  • Guarentee you a best-selling novel. If it could, I would be writing a best-selling novel instead of this software
  • Fix your grammar and spelling. It would be better to use a word processor for that.
To use the style helper, click on "Try it out!" below, then follow the link. Post your writing into the Style Helper, and let it go to work! We have found that it does best with 1000-3000 words at a time.
